Preliminary Constraints

1. Each account is identified by email. It must have a password.
2. Each pet owner is identified by an email. His/her name and telephone number must be recorded. Other information such as area and credit card numbers are optional. The email will reference the email of Accounts.
3. Each caretaker is identified by an email. His/her names, telephone numbers, rating and current number of pets taking care of must be recorded.  The email will reference the email of Accounts. 
4. A caretaker should be either part time or full time. 
5. Each caretaker needs to specify his or her own availability in time.
6. Each PSC admin is identified by respective emails. He/she must have a password.
7. A pet owner may own multiple pets, and each pet is dependent on a single pet owner. 
8. Pets are identified by pet id generated by the system. A pet must fit into some category and have a name. Special requirements, age and gender are optional for pets.
9. Each category of pets has a base price set by the clinic.
10. Each caretaker is capable of taking care of at least one category of pets, each with a daily price.
11. Each caretaker is paid a salary for a given month. 
12. A Pet owner can bid for a caretaker to care for a pet he or she owns with a price offered. The bid must record the start day and the duration given by the pet owner. Multiple pet owners can bid for the service of one caretaker. A pet owner can also bid for multiple caretakers.
13. If a bid is successful, a transaction starts. 
14. Each transaction requires a transfer method (delivering, picking up or meeting at the physical building of PCS).
15. Each transaction requires a payment method (credit card or cash).
16. After a transaction, pet owners can leave ratings and reviews for the caretaker.
