Preliminary Constraints 
1. Each User is identified by email. And it must have a password. A user can be a pet owner, a caretaker and a PSC admin.
2. Each pet owner is identified by an email referencing that of a user. His/her name and telephone number must be recorded. Other information such as area and credit card numbers are optional.
3. Each caretaker is identified by an email referencing that of a user.  His/her names, telephone numbers, rating and current number of pets taking care of must be recorded.
4. A caretaker should be either part time or full time. Full time caretakers can take care of 5 pets. Number of pets part time care-takers can take care depends on ratings.
5. Each caretaker needs to specify his or her own availability in time.
6. Each PSC admin is identified by an email referencing that of a user.
7. A pet owner may own multiple pets, and each pet is dependent on a single pet owner. 
8. Each pet is identified by its name. A pet must fit into some category and have a name. Special requirements, age and gender are optional for pets.
9. Each category of pets has a base price set by the clinic.
10. Each caretaker is capable of taking care of at least one category of pets, each with a daily price.
11. Each caretaker is paid a salary for a given month of a given year. 
12. A pet owner can bid for a caretaker to care for a pet he or she owns based on the caretakerâ€™s availability. A pet owner should provide a price for the bid. The bid must record the start day and the duration given by the pet owner. Multiple pet owners can bid for the service of one caretaker. A pet owner can also bid for multiple caretakers.
13. If a bid is successful, it also needs to record a transfer method (delivering, picking up or meeting at the physical building of PCS) and a payment method (credit card or cash).
14. After a successful bid, pet owners can leave ratings and reviews for the caretaker.
